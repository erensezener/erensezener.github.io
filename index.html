Research Interests

My research interests lie in the intersection of Machine Learning, Computational Neuroscience, and Robotics.
My long term goal is to contribute to the realization of the long-standing vision of artificial intelligence by developing fully autonomous robots that possess human-level capabilities. To achieve this, I would like to investigate the principles of intelligent behavior in biological agents and develop machine learning techniques using theoretically well-founded methods such as reinforcement learning. Nowadays, I am working on Inverse Reinforcement Learning.
